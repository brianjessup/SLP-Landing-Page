Startup Leadership Program

A non-profit, non-equity, training program for founders and CEOs of startups.

About SLP San Diego

Founded in 2008, the San Diego chapter of Startup Leadership Program accepts 25 Fellows per year into its global, world-class training program.

Over 6 months, Fellows are exposed to the basic building blocks of launching, growing and scaling a startup through mentoring by leaders in the community.

SLP is a non-profit and takes no equity in your startup and does not require a startup to apply.
