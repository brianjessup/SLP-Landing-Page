SLP San Diego - Fellow Referral Contest

We’re calling out to our past fellows to recommend high-quality candidates for our next year’s class. Our recruitment season is going very well - tracking 136% ahead of schedule! But we want to make this class the best of the best of the best, with honors!

Rules

Prizes

Besides that it’s the right thing for your friend to experience the amazing education, the local networking, and the lifetime global network, you should recommend folks to SLP because you could win:

  • Big Winner: Amazon Echo or Fitbit Alta or $150 Amazon Gift Card - your call!
  • Smaller Winner: Fitbit Alta or $100 Amazon Gift Card - your call!

How It Works

We are going to run a random drawing for the two prizes. Entries to the drawing are earned based on the below and you may earn as many as you like - each entry adds to the chances of you being selected as the big/smaller winner.

  • 1 entry / referred candidate who starts the application process
  • 2 entries / completed application by the candidate
  • 3 entries / selected fellow

Just make sure they reference you on the application form to get your entries recorded! Have them list you in the "recommendations" section or question that asks "Name any SLP Fellow you know or have met recently".

Who Can Participate

This contest is limited to the 200 past fellows who've graduated SLP in San Diego (and who aren't the current PLs). Those are good odds!

What?!?!

If you recommend at least one person who completes their application, you get a snazzy SLP t-shirt! That’s right - no entries, no drawing - recommend someone who completes the application, get a t-shirt.

Applications are closing August 15th! Make sure to get your recommendations in soon!
